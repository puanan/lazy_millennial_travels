Landmarks & Where to take a break!

Central Park

  • Have a classic NYC brunch at Sarabeth's
  • Cool down with an ice cream sandwich from Coolhaus
  • Have the most satisfying chocolate chip cookie you'll ever try at Le Vain Bakery

The Brooklyn Bridge

  • Enjoy margaritas and tacos in the patio at Big Star across the Bridge
  • Cool down after the walk with a scoop at Ice and Vice
  • Start your day with Dim Sum Brunch at the iconic Nom Wah Tea Parlor

Time Square - if you really must!

  • The eternally famous falafels at Maoz won't let you down!
  • Not exactly un-touristy, but delicious nevertheless - Halal Guys chicken and rice
  • Catch a late night comedy gig for $10 or less at the Upright Citizens Brigade on 42nd and 11th
